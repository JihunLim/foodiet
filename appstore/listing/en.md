## Description — max 4,000 chars

**foodiet — your AI diet partner, one photo at a time.**

If logging a meal still takes you 30 seconds of searching menus, typing grams, and picking a meal slot — stop. foodiet's AI assistant Foodie reads the photo and fills it in for you. Dish names, portions, calories, macros, and meal timing — all automatic.

■ What it does

• One-tap photo logging
  — Just snap. The AI identifies each item and estimates calories and portion.
  — Breakfast, lunch, dinner, or late-night? It's classified automatically from time and photo cues.

• Macro breakdown
  — Carbs, protein, fat in grams and percentages, at a glance.
  — A confidence score tells you how sure the AI is.

• Share-meal math
  — Ate with friends? Just pick "n people" and foodiet divides the total into your share.

• Daily coach — Foodie
  — A short, warm coaching note comparing today's intake to your goal.
  — No shaming on off days. Just honest, soft feedback.

• Home widget
  — Today's remaining kcal right on your home screen.
  — Tap the widget to open the camera instantly.

• One-card day share
  — Send your PT, trainer, or nutrition coach a single image with today's meals.
  — Works for any past date from the History tab.

• Weight tracking + future projection
  — Log your weight in two taps. Each entry plots as a dot on a chart.
  — A dashed line projects your weight to your target date based on your last
    14 days of intake versus your TDEE.
  — Verdict: 🎯 on track, ⚠️ tight, or 🔴 off track — with concrete advice
    ("cut N more kcal/day" or "extend the deadline by D days").

• Insights
  — Weekly and monthly calorie trends, meal-type distribution, your most-eaten items.

■ Who it's for

• Anyone logging meals for a PT or trainer
• People who want calorie awareness without typing everything
• Diet-app quitters who gave up on manual menu input
• Anyone who wants a gentle daily check-in instead of a nagging app

■ Sign-in

• One-tap with Apple, Google, or Kakao.
• No passwords to remember.

■ Privacy

• Photos live in private Supabase Storage with signed URLs — only you can see them.
• Ad personalization (IDFA) is optional. Decline it and every feature still works.
• Full policy: Settings > Privacy Policy.

■ Pricing

• 1.0 is free. You'll see a small native ad in the Insights tab.
• A premium tier (ad-free + advanced coach) is planned for later releases.

■ Languages

• Korean (primary)
• English
